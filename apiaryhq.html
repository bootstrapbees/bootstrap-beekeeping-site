Apiary HQ

Your Apiary.
Fully Managed.

Apiary HQ is a beekeeping management app built from the ground up by a beekeeper, for beekeepers. Everything you need to run your apiary — in one place, on any device.

📊 Dashboard

Everything at a Glance

The dashboard gives you a live snapshot of your apiary — current weather, fly score, pollen forecast, and hive status all in one view. Know before you go whether conditions are right for an inspection.

🔍 Inspections

Track Every Frame

Log inspections with queen status, population, brood, honey stores, feeding records, and notes. Export to PDF or CSV. Compare hives side by side. Your inspection history, always at hand.

🧭 Guided Walkthrough

Step by Step, Every Time

Choose a guided walkthrough for step-by-step inspection guidance, or quick entry when you know what you are doing. Built for new beekeepers and experienced hands alike.

⚙️ Everything Included

Built to Run Your Whole Operation
