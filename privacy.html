Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 20, 2026  ·  Bootstrap Beekeeping

Plain-English summary: We collect only what we need to run this site and respond to inquiries. We don't sell your information, serve ads, or share your data with third parties for marketing purposes.

1. Who We Are

Bootstrap Beekeeping is a beekeeping education and tools project based in Cherokee County, Alabama. You can reach us at admin@bootstrapbeekeeping.com.

2. Information We Collect

  • Email address — if you subscribe to our mailing list via the homepage form.
  • Contact inquiries — any information you include when emailing us directly.
  • Analytics data — anonymous usage data via Google Analytics 4 (pages visited, time on site, general location). This data is aggregated and not linked to your identity.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To send email updates when new posts are published (subscribers only)
  • To respond to direct inquiries
  • To understand how visitors use the site via anonymous analytics

We do not use your information to serve advertisements or sell it to third parties.

4. Email Subscriptions

If you subscribe, your email is stored in our Supabase database. You can unsubscribe at any time by emailing admin@bootstrapbeekeeping.com and we will remove you promptly.

5. Third-Party Services

  • Supabase — email subscriber storage
  • Cloudflare Pages — site hosting and CDN
  • Google Analytics 4 — anonymous site analytics
  • Resend — transactional email delivery

6. Cookies

This site uses cookies set by Google Analytics to track anonymous usage. No personal information is stored in cookies. You can disable cookies in your browser settings.

7. Data Retention

Subscriber emails are retained until you request removal. To request deletion of your data, email admin@bootstrapbeekeeping.com.

8. Children's Privacy

This site is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time and will update the effective date at the top. Continued use of the site constitutes acceptance.
